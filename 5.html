What to do?


Eat out at Grumpy Joe's

Grumpy Joe is among the most well-loved restaurants in Baguio City. Locals, tourists, and temporary residents alike find comfort in their hearty servings of US and Italian food, particularly their pizza and pasta.

Visit the Diplomat Hotel

This eerie abandoned building with a dark history is said to be one of the most haunted places in the Philippines

Try Here!

Pick your own strawberry at the farm

If you are in Baguio, you need to go outside of the city and visit this famous strawberry farm in La Trinidad, the capital of Benguet. You will see the country’s most colorful fields and delicious strawberries.

Visit Local Residences

Enjoy the colorful houses of local residences and take instagrammable pictures to show your friends! Also connect with locals and explore the hidden gems in baguio!

Try Here!

Enjoy the Panagbenga Festival

Panagbenga is an annual flower festival celebrated every February which takes place in Baguio City, Philippines. The term “Panagbenga” comes from a Kankanaey term meaning “season of blooming”. This festival reflects the history, traditions and values of Baguio and the Cordilleras. It lasts over a month long and flourishes through community spirit involvement.
